I am a Brazilian biologist and writer. Currently a postdoctoral researcher based in Helsinki, Finland, working with semantic modelling, critical archival studies, museum studies, and videogame studies.

As a biologist, my interests reside in specialized terminology, archival science, and natural history/biological collections' studies. I am also interested in relational and semantic data models, database theory, data provenance and transformation, and the ethics of datafication.

As a writer, my inspirations lie in speculative and weird fiction, such as Octavia E. Butler, Ursula K. Le Guin, Gene Wolfe, Jorge L. Borges, Samuel R. Delany, and Mariana Enriquez, among others.
